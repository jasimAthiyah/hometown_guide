Visit Hong Kong

Enjoy culture and sightseeing in this vibrant country.

Top three activities to do at Hong Kong

Hike the Dragon's Back

Filled with impressive coastal views and scenic views without too much incline and climbing.

Try the famous pineapple bun

A flaky, crunchy sweet crust atop the fluffy bun complete with a thick, cold pat of butter that quickly melts. A true delicacy!

Watch the symphony of Lights

A nightly light and sound show in which 42 buildings and attractions participate with music, laser lights and firework displays.
